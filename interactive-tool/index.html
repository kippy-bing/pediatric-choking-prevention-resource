Response Techniques

Step 1 of 5
Position the child safely
For infants: Place infant face-down along your forearm, head lower than chest, supporting head and jaw with your hand.

Visual demonstration image

Key Points

  • Always call emergency services first
  • For infants, NEVER perform abdominal thrusts
  • Deliver firm, quick blows/thrusts
  • Continue until object is expelled or child becomes unresponsive
Remember: If alone with a choking child, perform 2 minutes of care before calling for help.
